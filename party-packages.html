Rules & Regulations

Jungle Boogie is a single party room operated at a 2-hour time scale or 2.5-time scale. We do one party at a time, so your guest enjoy one hour and fifteen minutes in our inflatable and play area and 45 minutes in our party room, a full 2-hr party. We create memories for all occasions such as kids’ birthdays parties, play dates, baby showers, special events, and more.

 

  All parties are based on current reservations and availability. A $50 non-refundable deposit is made at the time of booking a party. Without the deposit, your date and time slot will not be guaranteed. This deposit will be applied towards your Final Purchase Price. We will contact you to confirm your reservations and collect Deposit. Sorry no checks
