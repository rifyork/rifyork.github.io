  • Jungle Boogie is designed for children 6 and under.  We reserve the right to refuse admittance to any child/ren over that age and/or do not fit the height and size requirement of the facilityâ€™s attractions.

  • Parents/guardians assume full responsibility and liability for children while on the premises. Do not leave your child unattended.
  • A waiver of liability must be signed.
  • We are a wrist-band security facility at Jungle Boogie. Children and adults must wear wristbands for security purposes (Open Play only)
  • No rough play, fighting or bullying.
  • No sick children or adults, please. Out of respect for others, if you or your children are sick please come back when both of you are healthy.

  • We are not responsible for loss, theft or damage of your personal property. Do not leave valuables or personal belongings unattended.
  • Jungle Boogie is a nonsmoking and non-alcohol center.
  • No sleeping on the premises.
  • Toddler area is for children 3yrs. And under.
  • No gum in the carpet area
  • Participants should use the equipment in a safe and responsible manner to prevent personal injury and property damage.
  • Management has the right to ask you to leave if they feel your child is being disruptive or causing harm to others
  • Access to the equipment is restricted to those who meet the minimum age requirement
  • Jewelry and loose article should be removed before entering the play area.
  • Please alert staff to any medical condition that may require assistance
  • Jungle Boogie reserves the right to request anyone who violates these rules to leave the facility immediately.
  • Appropriate behavior and language is expected from everyone at all times.
  • Jungle Boogie reserves the right to limit the number of people that occupy any area of the facility. Guests may be asked to wait outside the facility to remain within safe occupancy limits and will be admitted at Managementâ€™s discretion.
