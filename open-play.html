Rules & Regulations

Open Play

 

SOCKS are required to be worn by all guests in all carpeted areas of the facility.

 

*PLEASE NOTE: We require all parents/guardians to sign a waiver at Jungle Boogie.

 

 

Please be informed our Facility is by reservations only.

All Open Play, Parties and Special events require reservations.

Availability is limited for any Open Play time slot. (No Food during Open Play.) Open play is not private

(Pizza, cake and ice cream are allowed with party room rental Only!

(There are no tables except in Party Room)

Special Events, Open Play are open to the Public. However, they require reservations to prevent overcrowding.

 

All parties are based on current reservations and availability.

Entry is free for supervising Adults.

***Parents and guardians must supervise their children to ensure their safety at all times.
